Systematic investment plans

Systematic Investment plans (SIPs) are simple and effective mode of investing in mutual funds and does away with the need of timing the market. The SIP Investment process involves the customer investing a certain pre-determined amount in a specific mutual fund scheme on a regular basis – be it weekly, monthly, quarterly etc.

Invest a fixed amount on a weekly, monthly, quarterly basis and get benefits of high returns from equity to build long term wealth.

Why start a SIP?

Higher potential returns than FD

SIP has the potential of giving you higher returns in the long term compared to FDs and RDs.

Potential long-term gains

Rupee cost averaging and compound interest ensures that you get better long-term benefits compared to one-time investments.

Potential to beat inflation in the long run

Over the long term, SIPs have the potential to beat the rate of inflation which eats away into your savings.
