How Digital Gold works

Buying Process

Time is money! Be it buying or selling gold online, we offer you a simple and quick 2-step buying process. So how you buy pure Gold online through Aaditya Wealth?


01. Log In

Log in to our customer portal to purchase Digital Gold using your mobile number. If you are a new customer, you will have to sign up with us.

02. Enter the Amount

Proceed to purchase digital gold either in grams or INR.

03. Buy in one-click

Review your order and place order using UPI/Netbanking within the 5-minute price window.

04. Secured and insured

Your gold locker will be updated, where you can view your purchased Digital Gold holdings


Selling Process

01. Log In

Log in to our customer portal using his/her mobile number.

02. Enter units to sell

Enter the amount of gold you wish to sell either in grams or rupees. Also, provide your bank details to enable payment to be credited. That’s all you need to do to sell gold online.

03. Sell in one click

Review and place your sell request within the 5-minute price window.

04. Request Confirmed

The Gold Locker will be updated and the amount will then get credited in your bank account in 72 business hours.


Redeem Gold Process

Planning on gifting gold coins to your family and friends and want to redeem gold purchase online through Aaditya Wealth? Follow these simple steps and have your gold coins delivered right to your doorstep!


01.

Log in or sign-up using your mobile number to redeem your digital gold. You can redeem your gold in grams only.

02.

Choose a gold coin/bar from a list of options to redeem. You can add multiple gold coins online based on how much gold you plan on redeeming. Once done, add this to your online cart.

03.

You will be shown the coin minting charges.

04.

Confirm your delivery address.

05.

You will be directed a payment gateway. Proceed to pay the charges for coin minting and delivery.

06.
